About Me

I am a digital marketer and blossoming software developer. I am passionate about creating beautiful websites or applications that leave a lasting impression on the customer.

My coding journey started with an intense 12-week bootcamp from General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive Program. The skills I have learnt and the applications I have built have only made my love for coding grow. I would love to work with a group of like-minded people and continue to learn and progress in my journey as a software developer.

When not coding, I enjoy researching new foodie spots or things to do in Greater Sydney. Hiking is my adventure of choice and making little dollhouses is my secret passion - the frustration and then sense of accomplishment after finessing with the intricate details gives me a buzz.

I hope to bring my love for adventure, creativity and problem-solving to my new career in tech.

Projects

Tic Tac Toe

My take on the classic game, designed to be played with two people. The users can click on their desired icon and the application keeps track of their score.

Fukuzatsu

A P2P marketplace website that allows users to sell their pre-loved clothes. Users can sell or buy items by adding them to their cart and checking out.

RPG Character Generator

This project uses an API that generates items and accessories from the online RPG game, MapleStory, and allows users to create and customise their own MapleStory character.

Road trip planner

Designed with travelling after lockdown in mind, I wanted this app to be an all-in- one planner where groups of friends could plan a road trip. Features include a to- do checklist, directions on how to get your destination with Google Maps, and a cost calculator so you can divide expenses once the trip is over.
